Intro

Magpii Media is a small marketing and design agency based in Papillion Nebraska focused on serving retail businesses large and small to reach their short-term goals without losing sight of the long-term vision. Simply put, our mission is to help brands scale while maintaining the personal touch they bring daily to their customers and build lasting relationships with the best ones.

Next: Brand Strategy

Brand Strategy

Brands aren’t built overnight, so your strategy shouldn’t be focused on short-term tactics but rather sustainable growth. That said, it’s hard to stay strategic when your business has deadlines to meet today. From strategy to execution our goal is to help keep you from getting stuck in either extreme while pushing on both edges to help you get where you need to be.

Next: Content Marketing

Content Marketing

Mastering content creation for your brand on social media is no easy task. Hell, just keeping pace with your favorite channel is challenging enough. Our goal is to help you build a strategy using existing resources aimed at distributing one consistent message across all relevant platforms without neglecting the nuances of what makes each platform unique.

Next: User Experience

User Experience

While UX Design encompasses the entire user journey, it’s a broad and multidisciplinary field. Magpii is currently focused on improving the online shopping experience as well as creating a more seamless experience between multiple devices and stepping foot into the physical store. Here’s a few services we can help with.

  • Wire-framing & Interaction Design
  • Prototyping & Usability Testing
  • Market/User Research
Next: Virtual Assistance

Virtual Assistance

Unlike most agencies, we’re not afraid of getting our hands dirty and jumping right into your daily grind. In fact, we think it’s what makes the rest of what we do a success. Besides, sometimes you just need help freeing yourself up to work more on your business instead of in it, and creating tasks on some job board just doesn’t seem to take any less time. Whether you need help putting together the process or you’re bogged down by some repetitive task that only you seem to be able to do, we can help you unload the busy work so you can get onto the next thing that helps you grow. Contact us today to see how we can help.
